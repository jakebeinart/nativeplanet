TRANSFORM YOUR LANDSCAPE, NATURALLY

Native Planet specializes in sustainable landscaping solutions tailored to St. Louis. Let us bring your outdoor spaces to life.

LESS MAINTENANCE

Native plants require less watering, fertilizing, and pest control, which saves you time and money.

SUPPORT WILDLIFE

Native plants provide food, shelter, and breeding grounds for local wildlife, like birds, butterflies, bees, and other pollinators.

SUSTAINABILITY

Native plants are naturally adapted to the local environment, meaning they require less water and are more resilient against local pests & disease.

THE MAN WITH THE PLANTS

Nick Ciaramitaro isn't just the founder of Native Planet; he's a lifelong advocate for the natural world, armed with degrees in Biology and Horticulture. His dedication runs so deep that after graduation, he made the pivotal decision to turn down a full-ride to law school to chase his true passion: conservation.

Nick dove headfirst into making a difference, leading critical conservation and emergency response teams with Americorps St. Louis. After further honing his skills in the landscaping industry, he saw a unique opportunity.

He founded Native Planet to channel his expertise and infectious enthusiasm into transforming St. Louis landscapes, one beautiful, sustainable, native plant at a time.
