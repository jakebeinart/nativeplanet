LESS MAINTENANCE

Native plants require less watering, fertilizing, and pest control, which saves you time and money.

SUPPORT WILDLIFE

Native plants provide food, shelter, and breeding grounds for local wildlife, like birds, butterflies, bees, and other pollinators.

SUSTAINABILITY

Native plants are naturally adapted to the local environment, meaning they require less water and are more resilient against local pests & disease.

THE MAN WITH THE PLANTS

With degrees in biology & horticulture, you could say that native plants have been a life-long passion for Nick Ciaramitaro. After graduating from Mizzou and turning down a full-ride to law school, Nick realized his true passion was in conservation and spent the next few years pursuing that dream leading conservation and emergency response teams for Americorps St. Louis. After spending a few more years in the landscaping industry, Nick decided to take the leap and start Native Planet to spread his passion for native plants all around the St. Louis area.
